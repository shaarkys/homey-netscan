Know when the devices that matter are reachable and let Homey respond. Net Scan monitors phones, computers, appliances and other network devices, helping you create Flows when something comes online, goes offline, arrives home or leaves.

For reliable detection, give each monitored device a fixed IP address or DHCP reservation. Some battery-powered or energy-saving devices disconnect from the network while idle, which may temporarily make them appear offline.
